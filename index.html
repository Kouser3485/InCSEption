About The Hackathon

Welcome to the BMSIT CSE Department Hackathon, where innovation meets technology! This hackathon is designed to challenge your problem-solving skills, creativity, and technical expertise.

Join us for an exciting journey of coding, collaboration, and creation. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, this hackathon offers something for everyone.

With a diverse range of problem statements from AI to AR/VR, this is your chance to showcase your skills and win amazing prizes!
